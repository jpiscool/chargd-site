What's inside

🏠

Build-your-own home. Arrange and resize a dashboard of widgets — keep what you use, hide what you don't.

⚡

Charge. Today's momentum as a filling ring — it charges up as you finish to-dos and keep your habits, and reads "fully charged" when the day's done.

✅

To-dos that move. Add a task with a date, time, and reminder, flag what matters, carry unfinished items to today in a tap, and break big tasks into small steps.

🔥

Habits, your way. Build a habit or quit one, count-based or simple, shown as a visual heatmap and color chains — with streaks that forgive a rest day.

📖

A daily journal. A calm space to reflect, with a one-tap mood check-in, gentle prompts, and a writing streak. Look back on past entries anytime.

🗓️

Calendar. A visual day timeline plus week and month views, so you can see where your time goes.

🔁

Routines. Run a morning or wind-down routine step by step, at your own pace.

🔒

Yours alone. Optional Face ID lock, and export your journal anytime. Everything stays on your iPhone.

Privacy

chargd is built to need none of your data. There are no accounts, no servers we control, no ads, and no analytics or tracking SDKs.

Everything you create — to-dos, habits, routines, and journal entries — is stored locally on your device. It's only ever included in your own standard encrypted device backups (iCloud or Finder) if you have those enabled, which we can't access.
