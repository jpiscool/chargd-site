Frequently asked questions

Do I need an account to use chargd?
No. chargd works entirely offline with no sign-up or login. Just open the app and start.
Where is my data stored?
Everything stays on your device using Apple's on-device storage. chargd makes no network requests and collects no data. See our Privacy Policy.
Why does the app ask for microphone, calendar, or notification access?
These are optional and only used for the matching feature: voice capture of tasks (microphone/speech), showing your existing events next to day notes (read-only calendar), and reminders you opt into (notifications). You can turn any of them off in iOS Settings.
How do I back up my data?
Your chargd data is included in your normal encrypted device backups (iCloud or Finder/iTunes) when those are enabled.
